Meta-Memetics

Engineering Belief in Networked Environments
Aleph Station · November 2025
Read Paper (PDF)
Zenodo Archive · GitHub

Ideas often propagate on dimensions orthogonal to truth; we model those fitness dimensions explicitly. This paper operationalizes memetic fitness as F(m, E) = A · R · X · T − C, where success depends on assimilation ease, retention, expression drivers, transmission efficiency, and cognitive/social costs.

We introduce the Agency Index (Ag) to measure whether ideas are using people or people are consciously choosing to transmit ideas.
